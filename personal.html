TR3TA

Tr3ta is a board game created in 2010 (not yet released to the public) designed for three players. Each participant commands one of three armies competing for control of the central region.

The new app version stays true to the original mechanics but has been redesigned to deliver a more dynamic and intuitive digital experience.

With local multiplayer support, path guidance, animations, and customizable rules, the digital version aims to capture the original strategy while offering a modern interface.

NotixMe

NotixMe is a customizable smart reminder app featuring advanced scheduling, snooze actions, recurring events, random mode, and a long-term roadmap.

Built entirely in SwiftUI, the app focuses on robust logic and accessibility.

Upcoming upgrades include profiles/contexts, better UX animations, and deeper recurrence logic.
